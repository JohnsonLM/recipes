Cornbread:
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 cup self-rising flour
- 1 cup Milk
- 1 Egg
- 2 Tablespoons Sugar
- 5 Tablespoons Butter


Dressing:
- 3 Medium Onions
- 5 Celery Stalks
- 2 Cups Chicken Broth
- 3 Pieces Toast
- 2 Eggs
- 1/2 Cup Milk
- 10 Tablespoons Butter (save 2 for buttering pan)
- 2 teaspoons dried sage
- 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
